Backend Setup Required

The proxy needs a backend server to work. You can deploy one for free on Koyeb or Render.

Go to Settings and enter your backend URL, or set one up following the instructions in the proxy-backend repository.

Once deployed, the proxy will register a Service Worker to intercept and route your traffic through the backend server.
